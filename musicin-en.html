What is this App?

It is a work tool for those people who want to share their creations with the world and also for those companies that are in search of new musical talents.

What is a social network?

It is an internet platform that allows communication between users. The users can speak for messages, they can share information, photos, videos, etc. This publication can be seen by other users immediately.

What are we?

We are not Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, Snapchat, Tumblr, etc..., in this is an App you can be a content creator, you can post your creations and these creations can be seen by companies. These companies can meet you, if we had to compare with some platform, the platform would be Linkedin.

How to use?

  • We need to create an account, you must register with a nickname, email and password.

  • Your account must be verified with your email.

  • You must log in with your nickname and your password and enjoy!

Different profiles

Ordinary

  • All users will have access to the content of the application and they can access profiles of content creators and companies.

  • All users will have to create to profile, they have to choose if they are content creator or company

Content creator

  • The content creators are the only they can upload files to the App. They don't have a limit of files. These files must be type audio.

  • The content creators will have access to software of music edition, they must download the program and installer on the telephone or on the computer, the name of this program is TIKTAK.

  • The content creators must select the musical genre, type of voice and the musical instrument that they know.

Company

  • The companies will have access to advanced search engines, they can search for the profiles they need.

  • The companies will be able to download the content of profiles but they should first contact the content creator to download the file.

  • The App will have a chat system, the content creators and companies will be able to speak across this chat but only companies will be able to start the chat, the content creators will not be able to contact the companies.

What do you need to get started?

He App is totally FREE, the App wants to help contact content creators and companies. The App will not pay users or companies.
